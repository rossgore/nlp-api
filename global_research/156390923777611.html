Hitachi contribute to create a safer society through technology.
We are developing diversified systems with the various technologies in the Hitachi Group.

Based on its core technologies for the defense, aerospace and security and by mobilizing the technologies of the Hitachi Group, the Defense Systems Company contribute to build a society where people can live in safe and secured, by focusing on the social infrastructure security business.

  • Based on our core technologies for the defense, aerospace and security, we contribute to create a safer society.

    The Defense Business Unit has accumulated various technologies and experience related to safety and security through the defense business. Exploiting these technologies and experience, we will expand the range of security to cover the whole social infrastructure including cyberspace, and enhance our business with a global perspective. Today, we are facing numerous problems on a global scale such as cyber-attacks, terrorism, large scale disasters, global warming, environmental destruction, and in securing timber and other resources.
    The Defense Business Unit contributes to building a much safer society by tackling these problems in collaboration with the Hitachi Group companies to improve the security of social infrastructure.

  • Systems Engineer

    System engineers are expected to examine, analyze and assess markets, technological trends in Japan and overseas, and customer needs to make strategic plans for new business.
    They are also expected to utilize extensive knowledge to reflect customer needs, and provide feed back to the design and development divisions for delivering optimum products and systems.

  • Product Development

    Considering customer needs and environments in products to be used, we develop high-quality programs, systems and products using a variety of cutting-edge technologies. In order to develop and maintain highly reliable products, we perform several in-house and external tests. Even after delivery, we provide customers with various types of supports such as periodic inspections. We always examine customer requests in order to provide better systems and products.

Command and Control Solutions

To enable each SDF branch to operate at full capacity, Hitachi designs and develops the optimal systems that support decision-making at every level of command as well as education and training.　We also work to make situational assessment and decision-making more efficient by utilizing simulations and artificial intelligence (AI) technology, and are working to make information that utilizes data analysis and extraction technology more transparent.

Command and Control Support System
With centralized management and processing of sensor information that ships and aircraft collect, SDF can create strategic situation diagrams to share between forces, share information among commanders, and provide systems to support appropriate situational assessment and rapid decision-making.

Intelligence Information Solutions

We provide the solutions to support information gathering and analysis of information gathered so that policy-makers for the national security of Japan and Self- Defense Force commanders can use Internet information, satellite images, communications data and other types of information to make the right decisions faster.

Information Integration and Transparency Solution
Hitachi provides system solutions to collect, process and analyze information from various sources necessary to decision making, and make the results more transparent. We combine natural language processing, image and voice recognition technology and diverse technologies to help analysts automatically find associations among the information collected. We are also working on technology to detect precursors using Information integration and space-time analysis.

High-resolution Satellite Image Solution
We provide various analysis services tailored to your needs, in combination with information from the sale of satellite image data from the U.S. Company DigitalGlobe with the highest level of resolution from a commercial vendor to detecting changes between images and feature engineering as well as conversion to 3D data and other image processing and analysis. In addition, we are actively engaged in use of AI in analytic technology to automatically detect a variety of targets such as airplanes and ships.

Cyber Security Solutions

Cyber attacks are evolving daily, and Hitachi is deploying solutions for a cyber defense and secure IT infrastructure able at all times to respond to the latest trends.

Cyber Defense Solution
For the Ministry of Defense, we design and develop systems to support assessment of the cyberspace situation and cyber defense command and control, and training exercises, as well as provide malware analysis and support. We are also actively developing cyber defense solutions in OT as well as IT.

Information Leak Prevention Solution
We labor in product planning, design and development along the axes of malware detection technology cultivated in defense projects, encryption technologies for the entire hard disk, and forced communications data encryption. Hitachi is also committed to the development of cloud and control systems and IoT technology.

Social Infrastructure Security Solutions

We leverage advanced technology and rich experience accumulated in defense projects to support safe infrastructure inspection and assistance in largescale disasters through a variety of solutions to secure people's lives and protect the social infrastructure.

Drone Use Solution
We provide solutions that make people’s lives safer and richer through inspections to ensure safe infrastructure, assessment of disaster situations, installation of critical facilities, and logistics from the sky.
With artificial intelligence (AI), big data and other advanced technologies at our disposal, we are advancing drone utilization, said to be the life of the air industrial revolution, and building the public infrastructure to support it.

On-Board Ship Solutions

Information Processing System/Simulator for Escort Vessels and Submarines
We provide the systems that are the brain of the ship, performing the integrated processing of sensor information from sonar to estimate the position of the target and analyze its motion, manage and control weapon systems, and provide the training equipment for it.
Unmanned Underwater Vessel (UUV) Autonomous Support System (AI) We provide the equipment that enables use of AI to automate the system that is the brain of a UUV.

Network Centric Warfare (NCW) Solutions

Data Link
Hitachi contributes to stronger operational capacity by sharing voice, data, video, and other information required to execute strategies between the ground and aircraft.

Small Unmanned Aircraft (Drone) Operation
We develop a number of systems used on environmentally resistant drones. These will contribute to assessing the situation in a disaster, so that people can be deployed to search for victims who need help.

Multi-network connection devices
We are working to seamlessly connect disparate communication networks, whether wired or wireless, so that voice, video, data and all kinds of information can be shared. In addition to enabling communication between a wide range of telephone, industrial radios, transceivers and terminals, it will also be possible to connect different display devices and cameras for use as a means of sharing information. We also set up radio transmission equipment for connection between sites, so that when public networks are cut off in a disaster situation, independently operated networks can be secured as a means of information sharing between organizations.

Integrated Electric Propulsion System Solutions

We bring together the Hitachi Group's energy and electrical technologies, to develop integrated electric propulsion system for naval vessels with exceptional operability and energy efficiency that are in operation on Antarctic observation ship Shirase, among a variety of other vessels. Now, with a full range of IoTand AI at our disposal, we are advancing research on next-generation systems that improve the convenience of facilities management and maintenance to further increase energy efficiency as we strive to provide the latest solutions.

Electrical System for Naval Vessels
We manufacture electric systems such as magnetic degaussing to control the hull magnetism on minesweepers, 400 Hz power converters and other equipment for vessels.

Research on Power Systems for Next-generation Escort Vessels
We are investigating AI-type energy control systems for escort vessels of the future, as well as researching a wide spectrum of software and hardware technologies for DC power distribution systems.

Mechatronics Solutions

Hitachi is fusing vehicle and bridge mechanics technologies, material processing technologies used on the Shinkansen and electronics technologies to offer highly reliable mechatronics systems that ensure the execution of defense duties and fast recovery of lifelines and assistance in the event of a disaster. We are also promoting digital solutions by use of electric vehicles and unmanned control, as well as improved features/serviceability that take advantage of IoT and AI.

Global Expansion

Hitachi has been marketing satellite imaging services and disaster prevention systems not only to Europe and America but also to emerging countries.
We are applying the latest overseas technologies to our integrated systems actively.
