          Mine Warfare Systems

          SUPPORTING ALL PHASES

          CONTACT US

          – from search to destruction

          KONGSBERG’s Maritime Mine Counter-Measures (MMCM) systems support all operation of mine hunting or mine sweeping, from planning and preparation, via operation, to reporting and evaluation done either from dedicated MCM vessels or organic MCM systems.

          The MCM C2 system is fully integrated to all sensors and uses all available information, being historical-, tactical-, environmental- and sensor information to give the most relevant data to the operators. The MCM C2 system together with a KONGSBERG DP system makes it safe to operate within the minefield with low risk to personnel and equipment.

          The Norwegian coastline includes almost any type of environments and bottom conditions and the KONGSBERG systems have proven their capabilities in use on the Norwegian Oksøy class MCMVs.
