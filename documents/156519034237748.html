Chile Military Expenditure

  •  Summary
  •  Download

Military Expenditure in Chile increased to 5382 USD Million in 2018 from 5370 USD Million in 2017. Military Expenditure in Chile averaged 2369.35 USD Million from 1953 until 2018, reaching an all time high of 5382 USD Million in 2018 and a record low of 115 USD Million in 1954.
